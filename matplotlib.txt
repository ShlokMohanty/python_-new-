Data visualization is always helpful in understanding the data .
It is used to create multiple graphs.
>>>import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
x = [1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9,0]
num_bins = 5
plt.hist(x,num_bins,facecolor='blue')
plt.show()


simple plots in matplotlib:
1) Line_plot: plot()
2) Sub_plot: subplot()
3)images: imshow()
4)Histogram: hist()
5)Bar Charts: bar()
6)Pie Chart: pie()
7)Tables: table()
8)Scatter_plot: scatter()
